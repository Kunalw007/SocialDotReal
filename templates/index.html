Need of SocialDotReal

The number of social media users is increasing exponentially, but so is the number of fake accounts. Fake accounts are used to spread spam, provide false product reviews, publish false news, and even interfere with political campaigns. In business, fake accounts can cause significant harm by wasting money, causing reputational damage, causing legal issues, and many other things. Because of the enormous growth of the online social network, the number of fake accounts is increasing dramatically; thus, such accounts must be detected. A system for detecting fake accounts is required. Our website "SocialDotReal" aims to provide users with a platform for determining whether a social media account is fake or real based on the user's input parameters.
